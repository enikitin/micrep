About Micrep

Micrep is probably the first Russian representative company working with electronics, microelectronics, semiconductors. Focusing on semiconductors, we also support sales of power supplies and electromechanical components. Our activities are based on many years' experience in the market of electronic components, knowledge of the market, local customers and distributors. Located in Russia, Micrep represents manufacturers of electronic components in the territory of Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Moldova, Kazakhstan and other ex-USSR countries.
